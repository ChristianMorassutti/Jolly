The Jolly Process

Our 3-step process ensures that your Christmas light installation is a seamless and enjoyable experience, leaving your home or business shining with festive brilliance.

Step 1:

Consultation and Design

Reach out to Jolly Giant to schedule a consultation. You can do this by calling us or simply sending an email directly to us.


During the consultation, share your holiday lighting vision with our expert team. We'll listen to your ideas, preferences, and budget to understand your needs.

 

Our skilled professionals will create a custom lighting design tailored to your home or business. We'll provide recommendations and options to make your property truly festive.

Step 2:

Professional Installation

Once you're satisfied with the design, we'll schedule a convenient installation date.


Our installation team will arrive on time, fully equipped with top-quality LED lights and materials.

 

We ensure that the installation is safe, secure, and complies with all safety standards. Our experts handle the entire process, from stringing lights to setting up timers and controls.

Step 3:

Enjoy the Magic

After installation, all that's left for you to do is sit back, relax, and enjoy the festive atmosphere. We take care of everything!

 

Should you have any questions or concerns, our customer support team is just a call away.

 

After the holiday season, we'll return to carefully remove the lights and decorations. We can also offer storage solutions to keep your lights safe for next year.


Jolly Giant is dedicated to making your holiday season magical and stress-free.
